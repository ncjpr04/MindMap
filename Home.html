Magic Words for Your Heart

Let's Learn About Feelings!

😊

Happy

Happiness feels like sunshine inside! It's okay to feel happy even when others are sad—your feelings matter too.

😢

Sad

Sadness is like rain clouds—it's temporary! Drawing how you feel or talking to someone can help the sun come back.

😠

Angry

Anger is a storm inside. Try squeezing a stuffed animal or dancing to loud music until the storm calms down.
